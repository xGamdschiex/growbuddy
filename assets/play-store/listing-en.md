## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

GrowBuddy is your science-based companion for indoor growing. Whether hobbyist or pro — GrowBuddy helps you grow healthy plants and optimize your yield.

🌱 DAILY CHECK-IN
Daily check-ins with photo, temperature, humidity, pH & EC. Build your streak and earn XP with multipliers (2× after day 7, 3× after day 30). Local push reminders keep you on track.

📊 SCIENCE-BASED
• VPD calculator (Vapor Pressure Deficit) for optimal climate
• DLI calculator (Daily Light Integral) for precise light planning
• Drying timer with the 60/60 rule
• 9 established nutrient lines: BioBizz, Canna, GHE/Terra Aquatica, Hesi, Advanced Nutrients, Plagron, House & Garden, Atami, Hy-Pro

🤖 AI PLANT DOCTOR
Take a photo → diagnosis in seconds. Powered by Google Gemini. The Doctor automatically includes your latest check-in (climate, phase, grow info) for more accurate results.

🎯 GROW TRACKING
Manage multiple grows in parallel. Phases (Germination → Veg → Flower → Drying → Curing) auto-tracked. Notes, photos, parameters stored per grow.

🛡️ IPM (Integrated Pest Management)
Current, EU-compliant IPM methods. No neem oil — instead modern approaches like beneficial insects, potassium soap and sulfur.

☁️ OFFLINE-FIRST + CLOUD SYNC
Fully offline-capable. Optional Supabase cloud sync for cross-device backup. Photos in Storage (no base64 bloat).

🔒 PRIVACY
GDPR-compliant. Photos stay private (RLS + signed URLs). No tracking, no ads. AI requests anonymized.

✨ PRO FEATURES
Advanced AI Doctor features, unlimited check-ins, premium themes. One-time payment or subscription via Stripe.

📱 PWA + NATIVE
Install as web app (iOS/Android) or grab the native APK. Both versions full-featured, incl. home-screen shortcuts (Check-in, Doctor, Calc, Grow).

Free to use. Made in Germany. 🇩🇪
